1"""Click options for the `flow step scan` CLI. 

2 

3Copied from `inspect_scout._cli.scan.scan_command` (and its `@common_options` 

4decorator) so the flow step exposes the same flags as `scout scan`. Excludes: 

5 

6- `-T/--transcripts`: the flow step sources transcripts from its log paths. 

7- `-F/--filter`: collides with flow's own `--filter` log filter. 

8- `--results`: deprecated in scout. 

9- `--dry-run`: collides with flow's `--dry-run`. 

10- `--response-schema`: declared in scout but never wired to the callback. 

11- `--log-level`, `--display`: provided by flow as common step options. 

12 

13TODO: refactor inspect_scout to expose these options as a shared list and 

14import from there instead of maintaining a copy here. 

15"""
